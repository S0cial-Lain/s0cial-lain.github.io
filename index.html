Who I am

I'm a French security researcher and developer who lives at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity. My work spans from building autonomous AI agents that investigate threats, to developing OSINT dashboards that map digital footprints in real time.

I believe in building tools that empower people — not replace them. Every project I create is designed to augment human intelligence, not automate it away. I'm OPSEC-conscious by nature and open-source by heart.

When I'm not breaking into things (with permission), I'm building communities, mentoring newcomers, and pushing the boundaries of what local AI can do on consumer hardware.
